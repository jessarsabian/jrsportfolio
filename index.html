Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. I include revisions based on the agreed scope so the final workflow matches the intended process.

What happens if the automation breaks after delivery?

I can provide post-delivery support, testing, and adjustments depending on the project agreement or maintenance plan.

How long does a project take?

Simple workflows can take a few days. More complex systems with multiple tools, AI logic, or testing usually take longer.

Do I need my own tool subscriptions?

Usually yes. You should own the accounts for tools like n8n, Zapier, Make.com, Google Workspace, Airtable, or CRM platforms.

Can you build automations for my specific tool stack?

Yes. I can review your current tools and suggest the best automation path based on what you already use.
